  • Hey, hi, hello! My name is

    Charlie Wongpisethkul

    You can call me Won for short. I’m a Thai American illustrator, designer, and shape-shifter from San Francisco, California. I have a life-long history growing up in the culinary and hospitality industry, which trained my eye for nuance and detail. The spark behind my work comes from the mundane and peculiar stories of the human experience — whether lived or learned.

    I bring stories to life through illustration, graphics, motion, and visual development. Send me a message–I’ll make it worth a thousand words.
